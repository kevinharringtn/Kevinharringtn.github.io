BREWING

A guide to different ways to brew our roasted coffee!

There are an endlesss amount of ways to enjoy coffee!

There are very many ways to enjoy coffee depending on what kind of coffee you like!

French Press

French Press is a style of making coffee enjoyed for generations by coffee lovers of all kinds. You leave the beans coarsely ground in water for four minutes, and then use the plunger to contain the grinds. This gives the coffee an intense and strong flavor and consistency that people love.

Drip

Debatably the most popular style of brewing coffee is the Drip coffee maker. This is where you place coffee grounds in a filter, and let hot water rinse through the grinds slowly making a great and clean cup of coffee. This is what the coffee maker many people use at home is called.

Espresso

This is a very famous and intense style of coffee. Invented in Italy to shorten coffee breaks during factory shifts, Espresso is made most often in machines with intense pressure. The coffee that comes out is in very small doses, and has less caffeine than regular coffee (common misunderstanding!) But is delicious and has a VERY strong taste.
