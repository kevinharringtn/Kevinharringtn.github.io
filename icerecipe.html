Iced Coffee Recipe

Here we have a recipe for those who want to try making iced coffee at home! This is an easy way to save money and learn more about the coffee brewing process.

To make delicious iced coffee here is all you need to do:

First, place 425 grams of ice in your brewer. Next, grind and place 96 grams of coffee in your coffee maker. After your coffee is ground, add 680 grams of water to your coffee maker. As the coffee brews, swirl the carafe around to melt the ice. Now enjoy!
