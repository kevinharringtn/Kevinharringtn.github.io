COFFEE

A look into the growth and work that goes into every cup of coffee that we so regularly enjoy.

There is so much to know and so many different ways to enjoy coffee!

Whether you drink decaf, hot, iced, black, espresso or any other style of coffee, each cup has a rich backstory and history.

Learn More

Sourcing

Sourcing is the first step to enjoying coffee. This involves acquiring coffee beans from various regions across the world where coffee is successfully farmed. Some of the most successful coffee farming regions are:

  • Africa
  • South America
  • Indonesia

Roasting

The next step in enjoying coffee is roasting the beans. Once the beans are sourced and imported, they must be roasted before they can be brewed. You can find a few different type of roasts, such as:

  • Light Roasts
  • Medium Roasts
  • Dark Roasts

Brewing

There are a variety of ways to brew coffee, and each one will result in a different tasting coffee!

  • French Press
  • Pour Over
  • Drip
  • Espresso
  • Cold Brew
